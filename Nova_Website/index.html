Core Modules
What Nova Can Do

Powerful commands, simple words.

Just type or speak naturally. Nova understands your intent and responds in both voice and text.

🔍
Web & Wikipedia Search
Search Google or get Wikipedia summaries — all read aloud to you instantly.
🌐
Open Any Website
Say "open YouTube" or "open GitHub" — Nova launches it in your browser.
🔊
Voice Responses
Every reply is spoken aloud using both browser TTS and pyttsx3 on the server.
🎤
Mic Input
Click the microphone and speak your query — no typing needed.
🕐
Time & Date
Ask "what time is it?" and Nova tells you instantly, with voice.
💬
Smart Chat
Greet Nova, ask jokes, say thank you — natural conversation built in.
